Command Legacy Beta — Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 25, 2025

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Command Legacy (“Company”) collects, uses, and protects information during the Beta program. By participating, you consent to the practices outlined below.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Basic sign-up data such as name, email, agency, and role.
  • Beta Feedback: Survey responses, interviews, bug reports, and feature suggestions.
  • Usage Data: Limited analytics such as page views, error logs, and crash reports to improve the Beta Software.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the Beta Software.
  • To analyze performance and identify bugs or usability issues.
  • To improve features and guide development decisions.
  • To communicate with you about Beta updates, surveys, or required participation steps.

4. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or tracking for commercial purposes.
  • We do not share your individual usage with your employer or agency.

5. Sharing of Information

We may share anonymized or aggregated data for product improvement, research, or reporting. Personal data may be shared only with trusted service providers that support our operations, under confidentiality agreements.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard technical and organizational safeguards to protect data. However, no system is completely secure, and participation in the Beta means accepting some inherent risks.

7. Data Retention

We retain Beta data for the duration of the Beta program and for a reasonable period afterward to analyze outcomes. Feedback incorporated into the product may be retained permanently in anonymized form.

8. Your Choices

  • You may request deletion of your account data at any time during the Beta.
  • Participation in surveys and interviews is required for continued access; declining may result in removal from the Beta.

9. Children’s Data

The Beta Software is intended only for verified law enforcement officers and supervisors. It is not directed to children under 18.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify participants of material changes. Continued participation after changes constitutes acceptance.
