About Command Legacy

Command Legacy is a private, officer-only platform that combines:

  • Police-verified forum – a trusted community where supervisors and officers share leadership advice, lessons learned, and professional experiences.
  • AI legal assistant – real-time guidance on state laws and policies to support confident on-scene decisions.
  • Leadership resources – curated content and peer discussion to help new corporals, field training officers, and sergeants succeed in their roles.

Access is limited to verified law enforcement personnel only. Participation is free once verified.

FAQs

Is Command Legacy free?
Yes, during this beta test all features are currently free for verified users.

Verification ensures discussions remain professional, private, and relevant to law enforcement.

Do I need an account to see content?
